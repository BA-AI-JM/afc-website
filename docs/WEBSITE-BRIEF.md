# Almighty Fighting Championship — Website Brief

## About the Client

**Organisation:** Almighty Fighting Championship (AFC)
**Type:** UK regional MMA promotion
**Founded:** April 2, 2016 (AFC 1 at Leeds Town Hall)
**Region:** Yorkshire, North of England, expanding nationally
**Tagline:** "Yorkshire's Best MMA Shows"
**Events to date:** 42+ (as of August 2025)
**Fighter database:** ~973 fighters across all events

AFC runs a mix of professional and amateur MMA bouts. Cards regularly feature up to 30 fights per event, making them one of the larger regional shows in the UK. They've been described as "the most exciting amateur fight show in the country."

The current website(s) are essentially placeholder-quality — ad-injected, fragmented across two domains, and don't showcase what AFC actually is.

## Website Opportunity & Recommendations

### What the new site needs to solve
1. **Single unified domain** — consolidate almightyfc.com, almighty-fc.com, and retire almightyevents.com references
2. **Event archive** — 42+ events with no proper home; fight cards and results should live on AFC's own site
3. **Ticket sales** — currently split across Skiddle, Wix, and fighter-direct; needs a clean integrated flow
4. **Fighter profiles** — 973+ fighters in external databases; even basic profiles would add massive value
5. **Championship showcase** — title history, current champions, belt imagery
6. **Content/media** — they clearly have strong photography (see this folder); needs a proper gallery
7. **Mobile-first** — MMA fans buy tickets and check cards on their phones
8. **SEO** — currently all search traffic goes to Tapology/Sherdog instead of AFC's own site

### Suggested Site Structure
```
Home
  - Hero with next event countdown + ticket CTA
  - Recent results highlight
  - Photo/video gallery teaser

Events
  - Upcoming events (ticket purchase)
  - Past events archive (fight cards + results)
  - Individual event pages

Fighters
  - Fighter directory (searchable)
  - Individual fighter profiles (record, photo, bio)

Championships
  - Current champions
  - Title history per division

About
  - History of AFC (est. 2016, 42+ events)
  - The team
  - Venues

Gallery
  - Event photography
  - Video highlights

Contact / Fighter Registration
  - Enquiries
  - Fighter sign-up for upcoming cards
```

### Design Direction
- Combat sports aesthetic — dark theme, bold typography, high-energy imagery
- The photography assets in this folder are high quality and should drive the visual language
- Reference promotions: Cage Warriors, BAMMA/Bellator UK, Brave CF for regional MMA site benchmarks
- Must feel premium but accessible — AFC serves both pro and amateur fighters

### Technical Considerations
- Consider pulling fight data from Tapology/Sherdog APIs or scraping CombatReg for historical results
- Ticket integration: Skiddle embed, Stripe direct, or a platform like Eventbrite
- Instagram feed integration for social proof
- Email capture for event announcements
